# R1002 — unmatched delimiter

**Message pattern:** `Unmatched delimiter: <)|]|}>`

## Cause

A closing delimiter `)`, `]`, or `}` appeared with no corresponding
open form. Common causes: one delete too many, a stray paste, or a
closing delimiter of the wrong kind (which closes nothing while its
partner stays open elsewhere).

## Example

```clojure
(+ 1 2))
```

The second `)` closes nothing.

## Fix

Delete the extra delimiter at the reported position, or — if a form
above it is missing its opener — add the opening delimiter where the
form should begin. Check that delimiter kinds pair up: `(`…`)`,
`[`…`]`, `{`…`}`.
